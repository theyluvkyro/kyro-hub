## Notes
- Images are sourced from TMDB. Need to prefix poster/backdrop paths with TMDB base URL (e.g., https://image.tmdb.org/t/p/original)
- The app forces a dark theme to match the requested cinematic Netflix-style aesthetic.
- VidKing player is used via iframe on the /watch routes.
- Wouter used for routing.
